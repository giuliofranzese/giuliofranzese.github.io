About my research interests

    In the past I worked on problems related to Statistical Signal Processing and Satellite Navigation.
    My current topics of interest are linked to Machine Learning, and in particular Generative Modelling and Bayesian Methods. My goal is to carry out methodological and theoretical research in those areas.
    The most recent topics I am investigating are:
    • Score Based Generative Modelling
    • Stochastic Gradient Markov Chain Montecarlo techniques
    My current position is supported by Huawei Tech. Paris- Netception Project
